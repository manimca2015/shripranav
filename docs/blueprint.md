# **App Name**: Fair Future Travels

## Core Features:

- Driving Tour Listings: Display upcoming driving tours with key details such as destinations, duration, and prices in an appealing card format.
- Month-wise Filtering: Allow users to filter driving tours by month to easily find tours that fit their schedule.
- Tour Detail Pages: Provide detailed information for each driving tour, including itinerary, accommodation details, points of interest, and booking options. This will incorporate embedded video from Cloudflare Stream.
- AI Tour Recommendation: Recommend driving tours based on user preferences, such as preferred destinations, budget, and travel style using a generative AI tool.
- Responsive Design: Ensure the website is fully responsive and accessible on all devices, providing a seamless user experience across desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep navy blue (#0A2351) for a sense of trust and professionalism.
- Secondary color: Golden tan (#A47B16) as an accent to evoke adventure and warmth.
- Background: White (#FFFFFF) to provide a clean and spacious layout.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif, for headlines. Body font: 'Inter', sans-serif, for body.
- Use modern and minimalist icons to represent different tour features and categories.
- Card-based layout for tour listings to showcase tours in a visually appealing and organized manner.
- Incorporate subtle transition effects and animations to enhance user engagement and provide visual feedback.
